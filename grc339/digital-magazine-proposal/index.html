Introduction

AIR//SHIFT

AIR//SHIFT is a digital magazine, focusing on freestyle snowboarding. It should encapsulate the reader with bold graphics and photos. The magazine will highlight rising riders, trick progression, park design, snowboard fashion, video edits, and the creative energy behind freestyle snowboarding. It will be for those who see snowboarding as more than a sport — it’s identity, expression, and movement.
