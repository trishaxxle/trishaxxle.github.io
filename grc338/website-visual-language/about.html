About Us

Starbent Strength is a powerlifting-focused training space built for lifters who thrive at the edge of their limits. Inspired by the idea of bending the stars — pushing beyond what feels possible — our gym blends hardcore strength culture with a cosmic, almost mythic atmosphere.

Here, every athlete is encouraged to break boundaries, hone technique, and pursue strength with intention. Whether you're chasing a new PR, perfecting your form, or stepping into powerlifting for the first time, Starbent Strength is designed to support your evolution.

We’re more than a gym — we’re a community of lifters who show up, put in the work, and raise each other higher. From chalk-covered barbells to meticulously programmed training cycles, everything at Starbent Strength is built to help you unlock your strongest self.

Lift heavy. Move with purpose. Bend the impossible. Welcome to Starbent Strength.
